# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard using Power BI

This project presents a comprehensive Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to transform raw business data into actionable insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, analyze customer behavior, evaluate product profitability, and identify market trends through interactive visualizations.

## Features

* Country-wise sales and revenue analysis
* Sales trend and performance monitoring
* Product-wise revenue and quantity analysis
* Customer purchase frequency and latency analysis
* RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) customer segmentation
* Revenue vs. sales quantity comparison
* Interactive dashboards with drill-down capabilities
* Data-driven insights for strategic decision-making

## Technologies Used

* Power BI
* Data Modeling
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence & Analytics

## Business Impact

The dashboard helps organizations identify top-performing products and regions, understand customer purchasing patterns, improve customer retention strategies, and make informed business decisions through real-time analytics and visual reporting.
